What To Do With Newbies

First class, done right

Which newbie walked in?

For a ClassPass member's first class, there is really only one thing you must get done.

The one thing that matters

Get them to sign the liability waiver

On the iPad, open their profile and tap the red "Liability waiver not signed" flag. Have them sign it before they start.

Why it matters

If someone gets injured and never signed, the gym is exposed. Clients have tried to sue before. This is the part you cannot skip.

Checklist

  • Open their profile on the iPad
  • Tap "Liability waiver not signed" and get them to sign
  • Take a photo if they are happy to. If you forget, no stress. Photo is optional for ClassPass first-timers.
  • No newbie report needed for ClassPass

A trialist is on a paid trial (right now it is 3 classes for $30). The plan does not matter, the steps are the same every time.

Checklist

  • Check their liability waiver is green (signed) If it is not green, get them to sign on the iPad first.
  • Take a picture of them
  • Write a newbie report
  • Post the newbie report in the WhatsApp trainers group

Example newbie report

Sarah Chen

Sarah is Megan's sister and today was her first time training with us. She loves running but doesn't do much strength work. She already knew a bit about F45 and really enjoyed the hybrid class today. She'll be back Thursday for a resistance class. No injuries.

A good report covers: their name, who they know or how they found us, their fitness background, how the class went, when they are back next, and any injuries.
