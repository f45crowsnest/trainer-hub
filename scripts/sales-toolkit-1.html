Sell On The Floor, Not On The Phone

Most coaches sell at the wrong moment.

If the client walks out the gym floor without signing, you've already lost them.

The best time to close a membership is right there on the floor. Before they walk out the door. Not in a follow-up call. Not in a text. Right there.

Why The Floor Is The Buying Moment

That moment, their brain is fully in your rhythm. They just felt the energy. They just trained. They're sweating, they're locked in, they're in your zone.

Every second you let pass after they walk out, you lose a piece of them.

They check their phone. Another gym's ad pops up. They start thinking the price is too high. They won't pick up your call.

What To Do Before They Walk Out

So this is what you do.

Before they walk out, lock it in.

  • Set the membership.
  • Run the payment.
  • Book their first proper class.

Get it done while they're still in the room with you.

Because now when they walk out, one thing is already done. One thing accomplished in their head.

The Payoff

99% of the time, they don't look back. At least not for a while.

That's how you sell.
