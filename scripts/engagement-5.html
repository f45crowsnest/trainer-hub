Engagement 5

Pairing People Up

Hook 0:00 to 0:12

Who you pair someone up with can make or break their whole class.

And most coaches never even think about it.

Members Engage Members 0:12 to 0:25

By now you know your members. Where they're from, what they like, what they're there for.

Here's the next step. Use your members to engage your members. Pair them up.

That's how a class turns into a community.

Three Questions Before You Pair Anyone Up 0:25 to 0:58

One. If you were in their shoes, who would you want next to you?

That's the whole mindset. You've got to actually care about their experience.

Two. What's the workout? Cardio, resistance, a competition? It matters.

Here's a real one. Two girls both want the 12kg dumbbells, and there's only one pair. So who do you pair her with now? A stronger guy, so nobody's fighting over weights? Or the member who just wants a chat and a new friend?

I'm not going to give you the answer. I want you to actually think about it.

Three. Social or solo? Don't guess. Just ask.

"Hey, do you want to start here, or are you happy going solo?"

And listen. If they say "I don't mind", that means pair them up. In my experience, the only ones who really want to go solo are the demanding ones.

Close 0:58 to 1:05

Ask the question, make the pair, and watch your class turn into a community.
