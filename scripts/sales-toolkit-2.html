Sales Toolkit 2

Selling Is Training, Not A Job

I know selling can feel hard. So I want you to flip it.

This isn't a job. It's training.

Life is about selling. Whatever you do, making money, convincing someone, communicating with people, it's all sales.

Three Things Before You Sell

Before you sell to anyone, think about three things.

  • Who is your audience?
  • Why would they buy it?
  • How are you going to sell it?

You've Already Done The Hard Part

Here's the good news. Selling to strangers is hard, you know nothing about them.

But you see your members every single week. You've already built the rapport.

That's the hardest part, done.

No Commission? Even Better

I know some of you think, "I don't get paid for this, no commission, no bonus." When I started selling, neither did I.

I looked at it the other way. Every conversation sharpened my skills. Every knock-back made me tougher. Someone gives me a dirty look? I can take it.

The Payoff

Then one day, when the sale actually matters, in your career, your business, your life, you're already comfortable.

That's the opportunity.
