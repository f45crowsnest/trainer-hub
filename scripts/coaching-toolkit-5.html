Coaching Toolkit 5

Confidence

Hook 0:00 to 0:35

The most qualified coach in the room is losing clients.

To someone who knows half as much.

Here is how it happens.

A member does a movement wrong. You see it. But you are new, so you stay quiet. You are not sure you have earned the right to correct them.

The newer trainer next to you does not hesitate. They walk over and coach it.

They get the thank you. They get the client.

Confidence gets believed before competence gets noticed.

So today, let us fix that. One mindset. Confidence.

Be brave to start. Be brave to finish.

Make Your Mistakes Now 0:35 to 1:00

When you have little experience, this is the perfect time to make mistakes.

Do not be afraid of them. Mistakes are what make you progress. Stronger. Better.

The Lawyer And The Doctor 1:00 to 1:25

The lawyer in the best suit is not always the best lawyer. The best looking doctor is not always the best doctor.

But the confident one leaves the best impression. Every time.

First impression lasts. Fake it until you make it.

Do It Your Way 1:25 to 2:10

So here is what you do.

Push your client the way YOU think it should be done. Do your style.

If they get pissed, if they get angry, sit back and analyse. But never give up on approaching them a second time.

Convince your client to take on your nutrition routine. Your training routine. Your fat loss method. Anything.

Back your method like it is the best one in the world. Because if you do not believe it, they never will.

What they think is on them. Not you.

Close 2:10 to 2:30

Do your job. Do it with confidence. And never give up.

I know it is hard. But I trust you. You can do it.

See you in the next video.
