Coaching Toolkit 6

Your Struggle Is Your Best Friend

Hook 0:00 to 0:30

Some kid walks in. Barely trains. Barely works.

Three months later, he passes you.

You've been training every day for years.

It's not talent. It's struggle.

Stop searching online for the perfect program. Stop chasing fancy exercises. Stop watching YouTube for motivation.

All of your focus should be on finding that one struggle point in your session.

Train the right way, not the hard way.

Today let's talk about it.

If You Can't Afford A Killer Coach 0:30 to 1:00

If you can hire a coach who kills you every session, congrats. You'll see results in no time.

(I'm only talking about training here. Not other stuff.)

For the rest of us without the money for a great coach, what do we do?

Find that struggle point.

Push Past The Limit 1:00 to 1:45

If you train in a group fitness class, push yourself beyond the limit every single time.

If you did 10 push-ups last week, go for 11.

When the struggle comes, don't tell yourself it's hard. It's always how we feel that stops us, not what we can actually do.

When the struggle comes, forget about it. Keep pushing.

If you really can't do it, let your body tell you. Don't let your brain tell you.

Train Till Failure 1:45 to 2:15

And finally: fail. Fail. Fail.

Embrace it. Welcome it. Your body learns from it.

Train till failure.

If You're The Coach 2:15 to 2:45

If you're a coach watching this, get your client to that limit. Get them to that struggle. Push them through.

They will thank you after they go home and feel sore the next morning.

See you in the next video.
